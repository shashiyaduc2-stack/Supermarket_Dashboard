# Supermarket_Dashboard
#  Supermarket Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project analyzes supermarket sales data to understand customer behavior, payment trends, product performance, and branch-wise sales. The goal is to generate insights that help improve revenue, customer experience, and business decision-making.

This dashboard simulates a real-world retail analytics scenario where data is used to optimize operations and maximize profitability.

---

##  Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance  
- Understand customer demographics and behavior  
- Identify top-performing branches and product categories  
- Evaluate payment method preferences  
- Assess customer satisfaction through ratings  

---

##  Tools Used
- Power BI (Dashboard & visualization)  
- Excel / CSV (Data source)  

---

##  Key KPIs
- Total Revenue (Gross Income)  
- Total Tax  
- Total Sales (Overall Transactions)  
- Average Customer Rating  

---

##  Dashboard Features
- **Sales by Payment Method** – Compares Cash, E-wallet, and Credit Card usage  
- **Revenue by Gender** – Analyzes contribution of male vs female customers  
- **Branch-wise Sales Performance** – Identifies top-performing branches  
- **Product Line Analysis** – Shows sales distribution across product categories  
- **Customer Ratings Analysis** – Evaluates service quality  

---

##  Key Insights
- E-wallet and Cash are the most preferred payment methods  
- Female customers contribute slightly higher to total revenue  
- Sales are evenly distributed across product categories  
- Branch C shows the highest overall performance  
- Customer ratings are consistently high, indicating good service quality  

---

##  Business Recommendations
- Promote high-performing payment methods for better user convenience  
- Improve performance of lower-performing branches  
- Focus marketing on underperforming product categories  
- Maintain high customer service standards to retain satisfaction  

---

##  Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Customer demographics (Gender, Customer Type)  
- Product categories (Product Line)  
- Payment methods  
- Sales, Tax, and Revenue data  
- Customer ratings
